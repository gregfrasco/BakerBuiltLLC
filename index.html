Our History

Baker Built is run and owned by Eugene Baker, a third-generation contractor. Eugene Baker has over 30 years of experience in the field of construction. Baker Built is located in scenic Bolton Landing in upstate New York and has its name on countless beautiful homes in and around the greater Lake George area. Baker Built’s hometown, Bolton Landing, is nestled in the foothills of the Adirondack park and Baker Built’s style fits right in.
